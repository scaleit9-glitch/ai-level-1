Prompting masterclass — 4 rules for great results

1. Give it a role›

Start every prompt: "You are a TikTok content creator specialising in AI tools for beginners." Context = dramatically better output every time.

2. Be specific›

Bad: "Write me a hook." Good: "Write 5 hooks for a video about earning €500 with TikTok Shop as a complete beginner — make them urgent and curious."

3. Specify format›

Tell it exactly what you want: "3-bullet list", "casual tone under 150 words", "table format I can copy into Notes". Format = control.

4. Iterate fast›

Don't restart if the output misses. Say: "Make it shorter", "More energetic", "Add urgency at the end". Stack improvements on top of the first response.

6 ready-to-use prompts — copy, paste & adapt

Script Generator
You are a TikTok script writer. Write a 45-second script for a faceless AI account in the [niche] niche. Hook must stop the scroll in 3 seconds. End with a clear CTA. Tone: energetic and direct.
Hook Generator
Write 10 TikTok hooks for a video about [topic]. Mix POV format, curiosity gaps, counter-intuitive statements and personal story openers. One hook per line.
Caption Writer
Write a TikTok caption for this video: [paste script]. Include the main keyword in the first 5 words, one curiosity gap, and end with a comment CTA. Max 150 characters before the cut.
Niche Research
I want to start a TikTok account in the [niche] niche. Give me: 5 content angles that are currently working, 3 underserved sub-niches, and the top 3 pain points of the target audience.
Brand Outreach DM
Write a DM to a brand offering AI UGC content creation. I make high-converting AI video ads. Keep it under 4 lines, professional but casual, end with a soft CTA to hop on a quick call.
7-Day Content Plan
Create a 7-day TikTok content plan for a faceless AI account in [niche]. For each day: content type, hook idea, and CTA. Format as a clean table I can paste into my Notes app.
Connects to Level 2
Every prompt above gets used in Level 2 (Marketing) — hooks become video openers, captions go under every post. Save your best outputs in your Prompt Library note (Lesson 1.7).

Standard editing workflow — do this in order every time

1
Import footage
Drag your Higgsfield/Hedra/Kling video into a new CapCut project. Trim dead air at start and end immediately.
2
Add auto captions
Tap Auto Captions — review and fix any errors. Choose a bold, readable style. Always on — no exceptions. Captions increase watch time up to 40%.
3
Add background music
Choose trending audio from CapCut's library or import from TikTok. Set music at 15–20% volume under the voice.
4
Add hook text overlay
Bold text on screen in the first 2 seconds. This appears before the viewer processes the audio — double-layer hook.
5
Add CTA card
Final 3 seconds: text overlay with your one CTA — "Follow for part 2", "Link in bio", or "Comment X below".
6
Export and post
Export at 1080p. Post from CapCut directly to TikTok or save and post manually to keep scheduling control.
